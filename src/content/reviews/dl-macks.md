If you're in Dallas and craving a memorable dining experience, D.L. Mack's is the place to be.

The Caesar salad is a MUST. The cornbread croutons are a tastefully unique touch that sets it apart from any other Caesar in town. Their signature martini is the "bleu and gold" — bleu cheese salt on the rim, which sounds odd and works completely. We ordered the Mack's Special pizza and it did not disappoint.

The ambiance is full Chicago Tavern — dark, convivial, unpretentious — and the menu reflects it.
