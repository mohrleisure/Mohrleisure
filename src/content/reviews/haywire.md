If you're looking for a true Texas dining experience, check out Haywire at Legacy West in Plano.

From the rustic-chic vibes to the rooftop patio and whiskey lounge, this spot brings elevated Southern comfort food and Texas hospitality together perfectly. Whether you're grabbing brunch, happy hour, or a date night dinner, Haywire delivers with locally sourced dishes, handcrafted cocktails, and one of the best atmospheres in North Texas.

Definitely a must-visit when you're in Legacy West.
