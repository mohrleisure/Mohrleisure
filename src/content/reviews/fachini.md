Italian restaurant in the heart of Highland Park Village.

Fachini combines traditional Italian recipes with fresh, high-quality ingredients in a warm and inviting atmosphere. From classic pasta dishes and wood-fired pizzas to decadent desserts, every dish is crafted with care. The restaurant also features an extensive wine list showcasing Italian and international selections.

Whether you're looking for a cozy dinner for two or a lively gathering with friends and family, Fachini provides the perfect setting to enjoy genuine Italian flavors and hospitality in Dallas.
