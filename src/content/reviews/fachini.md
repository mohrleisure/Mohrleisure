Our first time trying Fachini Italian at Highland Park Village — and honestly, it'll probably be our last.

The location is beautiful, the setting is classic Highland Park, and the drinks delivered (8/10 — no complaints there). The complimentary antipasti was a nice touch and genuinely the highlight of the meal. But beyond that, nothing stood out. The food landed at a 4/10, and for the price — also a 4/10 — that's a hard sell when Dallas has so many great Italian restaurants doing it better.

With spots like OSPI and Fachini's neighbors in Highland Park all competing for the same dining dollar, this one doesn't earn a return visit. Dallas's Italian scene is too strong to settle.

**The verdict:** Pass.
