Welcome to Dallas! If you're new to the city, here's our guide to the Dallas staples you absolutely have to try — and which places to skip.

## Tex-Mex

Dallas is famous for its Tex-Mex food. Don't miss out on classic dishes like sizzling fajitas, cheesy enchiladas, crispy tacos, and creamy queso.

**Go to:** Las Palmas, El Molino, Culpepper Cattle & Co.

**Skip:** El Fenix, E-Bar, Doce Mesas

## Barbecue

Texas barbecue is legendary, and Dallas offers some of the best. Try brisket, ribs, sausage, and smoked turkey.

**Go to:** Terry Black's, Hutchins, Cattleack

**Skip:** Pecan Lodge

## Frozen Margaritas

Cool off with a frozen margarita — Dallas has plenty of spots where you can enjoy this iconic cocktail.

**Go to:** Katy Trail Ice House, Joe Leo Fine Tex Mex

**Skip:** Mariano's Hacienda, Mi Cocina (unless it's happy hour), Mexican Sugar

## Steakhouses

Dallas is known for its high-quality steakhouses.

**Go to:** Tango Room, Goodwins, Nuri

**Skip:** Nick & Sam's (overpriced), Steakyard Dallas

## New American & Italian

**Go to:** Hudson House, Neighborhood Services, Terilli's, Bobbie's Airway Grill

**Skip:** Campisi's, Moxies, and avoid Deep Ellum restaurants

Enjoy exploring the flavors of Dallas!
